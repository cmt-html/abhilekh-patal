    • General Information.

      General Information:

      1. What is Abhilekh Patal?

      Abhilekh Patal is a digital repository curated by the National Archives of India (NAI) that provides access to a vast collection of historical records and documents.

      2. What is the purpose of Abhilekh Patal?

      The main goal of Abhilekh Patal is to offer easy access to India's historical archives, allowing users to explore and learn about the country's history, culture, and heritage. .

      3. Who can use Abhilekh Patal?

      The main goal of Abhilekh Patal is to offer easy access to India's historical archives, allowing users to explore and learn about the country's history, culture, and heritage.

      4. Is Abhilekh Patal affiliated with the National Archives of India?

      Abhilekh Patal is open to a wide range of users, including researchers, students, history enthusiasts, anyone interested in learning about India's heritage and past.
