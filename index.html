About Us

Abhilekh Patal Portal for Access to Archives and Learning is an initiative of NAI to make its rich
treasure of Indian Archival Records available to one and all at the click of a button.
Abhilekh Patal contains the reference media of more than 3.7 million files held by the National
Archives of India.
